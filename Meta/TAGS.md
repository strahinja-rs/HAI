# Tags Taxonomy

## Status Tags
- #status/inbox - just captured, unprocessed
- #status/processing - actively being worked on
- #status/seed - initial understanding, needs development
- #status/growing - developing, being connected
- #status/evergreen - mature understanding
- #status/draft - output in progress
- #status/published - finished and published
- #status/archived - no longer active

## Type Tags
- #type/source
- #type/concept
- #type/analysis
- #type/output
- #type/log
- #type/map

## Origin Tags
- #original - concept originated from human's own thinking (not from literature)

## Domain Tags (HAI-specific)
- #domain/relating - emotional, attachment, companionship, trust
- #domain/cognition - thinking with, cognitive effects
- #domain/creativity - creating with, generative collaboration
- #domain/agency - delegation, autonomy, authorship
- #domain/learning - skill development, education
- #domain/design - interfaces, UX, product design
- #domain/ethics - harms, risks, responsibilities
- #domain/safety - AI safety research, interpretability, alignment
- #domain/technical - implementation-level, architectural

## Evidence Tags
- #evidence/empirical - based on research studies
- #evidence/theoretical - based on frameworks
- #evidence/anecdotal - based on observation/reports
- #evidence/personal - my own experience

## Action Tags
- #todo/read
- #todo/process
- #todo/connect
- #todo/write
- #todo/review
