# About This Vault

## Purpose
This vault is dedicated to research and publishing in Human-AI Interaction (HAI)—the study of what happens when humans and AI meet at the personal level.

## Scope
- All modes of human-AI contact: relating, thinking with, creating with, delegating to, learning from
- Theoretical frameworks and empirical research
- Design and practice implications
- Original analysis and published outputs

## What's NOT Here
- Personal PKM / life management
- Client project work
- General notes unrelated to HAI

## Information Flow
Capture (Inbox) → Process (Sources) → Understand (Concepts) → Create (Analysis) → Publish (Outputs)
